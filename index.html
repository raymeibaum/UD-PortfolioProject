About

I am a forward thinking, self-started team player who is ready to pour passion into every project I touch. My greatest strength is being able to break problems into “byte” size (pun intended), approachable issues and work through them. I’m quick to admit my own ignorance, and am not afraid to turn to Stack Overflow and Google for the answers I need.

I wasn’t always a web developer. I spent the first part of my professional career working in the film and television industry as a camera assistant (IMDB). It was here that I learned how to work with a team of people in a high stress, high pressure environment to make something great. The film industry also taught me how to communicate well with my colleagues as well as my superiors, how to manage time efficiently, and to always pay attention to the smaller details.
